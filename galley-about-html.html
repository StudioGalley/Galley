About Galley

Galley is a creative studio that combines traditional printmaking techniques with contemporary graphic design. Founded in 2022, our mission is to create thoughtful, handcrafted work that bridges the gap between digital design and physical art.

As a printmaker and graphic designer, I bring a unique perspective to every project. My background in fine arts informs my approach to design, while my design training helps me create prints with strong compositional elements and visual impact.

Every print in our shop is handmade in small batches, ensuring quality and uniqueness. Our design services focus on creating meaningful visual identities and communications for brands and individuals who value craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Whether you're interested in purchasing a print, commissioning custom artwork, or working with us on a design project, I'd love to hear from you!
